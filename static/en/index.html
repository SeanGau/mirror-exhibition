Dancecology

Embrace

The dance video work "Embrace" is produced by Dancecology. Starting from the creative process of craft artists, the video pitched the topic from four sub-topics, which are Sound, Touch, Time, and Symbiosis. It documents the transition of the artist throughout the creative process, as well as the changes in body rhythm, perception and mood caused by different incidents, turning it into a combination piece of documentary and dance video to be narrated.
