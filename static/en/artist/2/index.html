Artist

PAN Tzu-Tsun (A-Dan)‧Tzun's Pan Sound Sculptures

Born Yilan, Taiwan.

In October 2013, having taught himself over the internet, PAN Tzu-tsun founded Sine. Studio, the first self-made steel tongue drum brand in Taiwan. Combining experience, design, and craft aesthetics, Sine. Studio offers musical instrument production more possibilities for life and leisure.
The studio has won the 2020 Taiwan Golden Pin Design Award, and the Cultural and Creative Boutique Excellence Award at 2021 Creative Expo Taiwan.

In June 2021, after further research, Pan produced the first Handpan in Taiwan. In August of the same year, to experiment and to improve his art, he started Tzun's Handpan, which started the process of Taiwan's first batch of hand-made Handpan.

In January 2022, Pan used the term of "Pan Sound" to integrate the cultural background and experience of making steel tongue drums and Handpan. The studio was renamed "Tzun's Pan Sound Sculptures."
